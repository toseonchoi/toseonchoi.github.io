Most Accurate Golf Rangefinders!

GolfBuddy Product Promotion

About Client

GolfBuddy is a leading innovator in the golf rangefinder industry with a primary goal to provide the most accurate and innovative golf rangefinders in the market. GolfBuddy has the most accurate course database with physically verified courses across the world.

Project Description

GolfBuddy publishes the featured product ads in PGA Magazine and distributes the e-blast to customers to maintain brand loyalty.

The advertisement mainly featured the product spec and information, announced the new line up, or emphasized the special promotion to encourage the sales rate.

I worked as an exclusive designer of GolfBuddy client. I created, executed, and delivered all creative materials to the client and the media. I helped out with TV commercials as a prop manager. It was my first experience of outside shooting and I learn how the outdoor filming proceeds.
