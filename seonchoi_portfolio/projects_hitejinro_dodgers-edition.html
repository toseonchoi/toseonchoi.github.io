Hite Beer Dodgers Edition Promotion

Background

Hite Beer has the partnership with LA Dodgers. Every year, Hite launches Dodgers limited edition for the LA Dodgers’ fans. By doing sampling event at the Dodgers Stadium, Hite Beer supports the field’s excitement, grabs the brand loyalty to the existing customer, also increases the familiarity, and spreads fame as the foreign beer.

Under this specific condition, this project is specifically targeted LA regional. As a graphic designer and LA Dodgers fan, I produced the various form of marketing materials, such as standing banner, poster, coupon, elevator wrap, t-shirt and announced the event through the social media. These mainly provide the new limited edition release, the local event information, also the general branding to raise its awareness.
