Go Blue! Go Hite!

Hite Beer Dodgers Edition Promotion

About Client

HiteJinro is No.1 distiller in South Korea and the world's leading producer of soju. The main products are Chamisul Soju—the world’s best-selling spirit & Hite Beer—the most popular beer in the South Korea.

Background

Hite Beer has the partnership with LA Dodgers and launches the special edition for the LA Dodgers’ fans every year. Therefore, this promotion is mainly targeted LA regional.

As a graphic designer and LA Dodgers fan, I produced the various promotional materials, such as standing banner, poster, newspaper ad, coupon, POP display, elevator wrap, LED signage, t-shirt, app ad, etc.

Also, our team actively announced the event through the social media. We provide not only the new limited edition release news, also the local event information.

By doing sampling event at the Dodgers Stadium, Hite Beer supports the field’s excitement, grabs the brand loyalty to the existing customer, also increases the familiarity, and spreads fame as the foreign beer!
