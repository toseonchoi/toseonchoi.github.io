Cedars-Sinai Outdoor Promotion

About Client

Cedars-Sinai is a nonprofit academic healthcare organization serving the diverse Los Angeles community. Cedars-Sinai serves more than 1 million people each year in over 40 locations, with more than 4,500 physicians and nurses and 1,500 research projects in motion. Cedars-Sinai is dedicated to providing care for everyone who needs it.

Project Background

Every month, GolfBuddy advertise their featured products at PGA golf magazine and send e-blast to existing customers to grab the brand loyalty. And for every year, GolfBuddy participates in PGA Merchandise Show which is the great opportunity of B2B.

I accomplished product-focused advertisements in various format; such as magazine ad, brochure, catalog, package, e-blasts, landing page, web banners, SNS graphics, and so on. It mainly featured the product information, announced the new line up, emphasized the special price, and encouraged the sales rate.

I also participated in PGA Merchandise Show and managed graphics for the show.
