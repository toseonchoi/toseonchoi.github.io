Cheong Kwan Jang Seasonal Event

About Client

Korea Ginseng Corp has been the global leader in the ginseng industry, and also the largest health supplement and government-certified company in South Korea. Mission of Korea Ginseng Corp is to make people’s lives better every day by providing them with the best, traditionally harvested, most carefully selected Korean Red Ginseng products to support health and well-being. Cheong Kwan Jang is a representative brand of Korea Ginseng Corp.

Project Description

Cheong Kwan Jang, primary brand line of Korea Ginseng Corp, offers the special event for the limited time throughout all seasons and occasions, including Korean traditional holidays.

Ads were executed in various format such as the poster, newspaper, standing banner, web banner, TV commercial, etc. Advertisement mainly emphasized the special price or giveaway, distinguished age group, and encouraged the sales rate.

This project targeted to the Korean and other Asian market and executed advertisements in various languages.

I've created various kinds of adverting material and delivered it to my clients and the media. I helped out with TV commercials as a prop manager. This project was a great opportunity to work with the cast and the crew.
