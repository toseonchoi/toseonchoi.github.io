Cedars-Sinai Specialty Campaign

About Client

Cedars-Sinai is a nonprofit academic healthcare organization serving the diverse Los Angeles community. Cedars-Sinai serves more than 1 million people each year in over 40 locations, with more than 4,500 physicians and nurses and 1,500 research projects in motion. Cedars-Sinai is dedicated to providing care for everyone who needs it.

Project Description

Nowadays, Korean consumers are looking for more reliable experts than traditional doctors in Koreatown.

I created the advertisements and published these in newspapers, magazines, and e-blast. The ads were distributed in Korean and English Bilingual format to target the larger number of consumers. The campaign provided information about the expertise and achievements of Cedars-Sinai in four major medical areas that many Koreans experiences.

The advertisement has increased the credibility of the Cedars-Sinai. Also, this campaign has increased the possibility that Korean consumers would consider Cedars-Sinai as their primary selection.
