Most Accurate Golf Rangefinders!

GolfBuddy Regular Subscription & Trade Show

About Client

GolfBuddy is a leading innovator in the golf rangefinder industry with a primary goal to provide the most accurate and innovative golf rangefinders in the market. GolfBuddy has the most accurate course database with physically verified courses across the world.

Project Description

Every month, GolfBuddy publishes the featured product ads in PGA Magazine and distributes the e-blast to customers to maintain brand loyalty. GolfBuddy also participates in PGA Merchandise Show with opportunities for business-to-business every year.

Regular subscription ads mainly featured the product spec and information, announced the new line up, or emphasized the special promotion to encourage the sales rate.

GolfBuddy's booth graphics theme for PGA Merchandise Show was the futuristic and high tech. Robots and surrealistic colors maximized the concept.
