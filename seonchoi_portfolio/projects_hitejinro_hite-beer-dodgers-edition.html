Go Blue! Go Hite!

Hite Beer Dodgers Edition Promotion

About Client

HiteJinro is No.1 distiller in South Korea and the world's leading producer of soju. The main products are Chamisul Soju—the world’s best-selling spirit & Hite Beer—the most popular beer in the South Korea.

Project Background

Hite Beer has the partnership with LA Dodgers and launches the special edition for the LA Dodgers’ fans every year. The special edition beer is only sold in Los Angeles region. Therefore, this promotion is mainly targeted the local residents.

As an LA Dodgers fan and graphic designer, I produced the various promotional materials, such as newspaper ad, poster, standing banner, POP display, LED signage, elevator wrap, t-shirt, coupon, app ad, etc.

Also I worked as a team. We actively announced the event through the social media. We provide not only about the new special edition, also the local event announcement and the game information as well.

By holding the sampling event at the Dodger Stadium, Hite Beer supports the field’s excitement, grabs the brand loyalty to the existing customer, and increases the familiarity. Moreover, as the foreign beer, the fame spread across the region!
