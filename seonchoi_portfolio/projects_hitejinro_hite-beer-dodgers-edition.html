Hite Beer Dodgers Edition Promotion

About Client

HiteJinro is No.1 distiller in South Korea and the world's leading producer of soju. The main products are Chamisul Soju—the world’s best-selling spirit & Hite Beer—the most popular beer in South Korea.

Project Description

Hite Beer holds a partnership with the Los Angeles Dodgers and launches a special edition every year for the LA Dodgers fans. This special edition beer is sold only in the Los Angeles area. Therefore, the promotion was mainly aimed at local residents.

Hite Dodgers Edition package design was focused on expressing the characteristics of the Dodgers. The simple modern design was loved by not only baseball fans but also by the general public.

Hite Beer promotion and Dodgers game information were provided through social media. Brand loyalty and familiarity were increased by hosting the sample events at the Dodgers Stadium. Hite Beer successfully supported the heat of the stadium and have spread Hite Beer's reputation as an easy to drink foreign beer.

The advertisements produced in various promotional materials, such as newspaper ad, poster, coupon, standing banner, POP display, LED signage, elevator wraps, SNS, app ad, t-shirt etc.
