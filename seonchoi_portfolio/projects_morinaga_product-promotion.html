Morinaga Product Promotion

About Client

Morinaga is a manufacturer of soy products. Morinaga produces the popular Mori-Nu Silken Tofu brand, a line of silken tofu widely sold across the US and around the world, as well as the industrial and food service ingredient Silken Soy Puree.

Project Description

Morinaga produces healthy, delicious, organic, and non-GMO products. Advertisements provided details information of the nutrition fact. Moreover, ads offered the recipes that fit the characteristics of each product to help consumers can craft great dishes, with a great ingredient.
