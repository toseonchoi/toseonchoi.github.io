Stylish Sparking Water Trevi!

Lotte Trevi Product Advertisement

About Client

Lotte is one of largest multinational conglomerates in South Korea and Japan. Lotte has become one of food and lifestyle business which provides the wide range of consumer-oriented products and services.

Project Description

Lotte sponsored South Korean travel-reality show, Youth Over Flowers, and placed Trevi product. They pushed forward the global marketing while TV show became popular.

Trevi marketing was extended to the print advertisement, and standing banner displayed on the market. The cool, thrilling, product image making maximized the advertising effect in the hot summer season.
