Ho! Ho! Ho! Holidays!

HiteJinro Holiday Edition Promotion

About Client

HiteJinro is the leading distiller in South Korea and the world's top producer of soju. Known for its flagship products, Chamisul Soju, the world’s best-selling spirit, and Hite Beer, South Korea’s most popular beer, HiteJinro has solidified its reputation in the global spirits industry.

Project Description

Each year, HiteJinro releases special holiday editions, one of their biggest promotions of the season. The advertisements featured holiday decorations, winter themes, and Korean celebrity IU to capture the festive mood.

I worked closely with the marketing department to promote the Holiday Editions as perfect mood makers and the ideal gifts for adults. Through social media, we encouraged customers to create unique Christmas trees made from soju bottles and participated in fun content and gift events.
