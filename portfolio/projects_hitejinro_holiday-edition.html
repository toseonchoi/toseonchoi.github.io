Ho! Ho! Ho! Holidays!

HiteJinro Holiday Edition Promotion

About Client

HiteJinro is No.1 distiller in South Korea and the world's leading producer of soju. The main products are Chamisul Soju—the world’s best-selling spirit & Hite Beer — the most popular beer in South Korea.

Project Description

HiteJinro releases the special editions at the end of each year. It was one of the biggest promotions of the year and was actively announcing the launch of the new package designs.

During the holiday season, the advertisements were used lots of holiday decorations, mood of winter, Korea celebrity IU to catch the eye of viewers, creating a sense of year-end.

Advertisements promoted HiteJinro Holiday Editions as great mood makers and as the best holiday gifts for adults. Through social media, HiteJinro suggested making a unique Christmas tree made from soju bottles.

I worked closely with the marketing department. We encouraged the customers to participate in HiteJinro social media by offering interesting contents and gift event.
