Skechers Landing Page

About Client

Skechers USA is a global footwear brand offering lifestyle and high-performance shoes for men, women, and children. The company is dedicated to developing and marketing top-quality footwear that caters to a wide range of preferences and needs. As one of the largest footwear brands in the world, Skechers USA continues to push the boundaries of innovation and style, while maintaining a commitment to exceptional quality and customer satisfaction.

Project Description

Sketchers aimed to provide users with a dedicated, informative, and user-friendly experience for a variety of purposes, such as membership and benefits, exclusive holiday promotions, limited edition releases, and more.

As a web designer, my role involved collaborating closely with developers and marketing teams to ensure the appropriate information was conveyed through effective user interfaces. My experience with UI design and web coding allowed me to deliver exceptional results that met and exceeded client expectations.
