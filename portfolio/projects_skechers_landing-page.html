About Client

Skechers USA is a global leader in lifestyle and athletic footwear and apparel for men, women, and kids, featuring innovative comfort technologies. Committed to delivering high-quality products, Skechers caters to diverse tastes and needs with exceptional style and comfort.

As one of the world’s largest footwear brands, Skechers continues to push the boundaries of style and functionality while maintaining its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Project Description

Skechers aimed to provide users with an informative and user-friendly online experience for various purposes, including membership benefits, exclusive holiday promotions, limited edition releases, and more.

As a web designer, I collaborated closely with the user experience team, marketing team, and developers to ensure seamless communication of key information through effective user interfaces. By leveraging my expertise in UI design and web coding, I delivered exceptional results that met and exceeded client expectations.
