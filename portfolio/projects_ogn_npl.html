OGN — National PUBG League (NPL)

About Client

OGN is the world's first professional eSport TV channel, division of CJ ENM. OGN has been writing eSports history for 19+ years. OGN created the world’s FIRST Esports league and continuously pioneered the world’s best eSports Leagues with world popular games — StarCraft, Starcraft 2, League of Legends, Overwatch, PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds, and more. OGN brought the love of video games and eSports to homes across 150+ countries.

Project Brief

The National PUBG League (NPL) is the premier PUBG competition in North America. Sixteen top teams battle in regular league play for a chance to qualify for international tournaments, special events and, ultimately, the PUBG Global Championship.
