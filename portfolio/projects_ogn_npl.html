OGN — National PUBG League (NPL)

About Client

OGN is the world’s first professional eSports TV channel, a division of CJ ENM, and has been a leader in the eSports industry for over 19 years. Pioneering the world’s first eSports league, OGN continues to set the standard with top-tier competitions for games like StarCraft, League of Legends, Overwatch, and PUBG. With its programming reaching over 150 countries, OGN has played a major role in bringing eSports to a global audience.

Project Description

The National PUBG League (NPL), organized by OGN, takes PlayerUnknown’s Battlegrounds to the next level with top players from North America competing in exciting events throughout the year. The league boasts a massive prize pool and helps drive the game’s popularity across the region.

As a graphic designer, I crafted various promotional materials for social media and YouTube, including event branding, player profiles, and tournament schedules. Real-time broadcasting added an exciting dimension, allowing me to showcase my design skills and contribute to the project’s success.
