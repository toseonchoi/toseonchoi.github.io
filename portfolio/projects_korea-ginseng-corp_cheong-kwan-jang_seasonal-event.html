Korea Ginseng Corp — Cheong Kwan Jang: Seasonal Event

About Client

Korea Ginseng Corp, the global leader in ginseng, is the largest health supplement and government-certified company in South Korea. Its mission is to improve lives by offering premium, traditionally harvested Korean Red Ginseng products for health and well-being. Cheong Kwan Jang is the company's flagship brand.

Project Description

Cheong Kwan Jang, the flagship brand of Korea Ginseng Corp, offers limited-time promotions throughout the year, including for Korean traditional holidays. Ads were created in various formats such as posters, newspapers, standing banners, web banners, and TV commercials, focusing on special prices, giveaways, and targeting specific age groups to drive sales.

This project targeted both the Korean and broader Asian markets, with advertisements in multiple languages. I created various advertising materials and delivered them to clients and the media. Additionally, I assisted with TV commercials as a prop manager, gaining valuable experience working with the cast and crew.
