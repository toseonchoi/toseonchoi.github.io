Go Blue! Go Hite!

Hite Beer Dodgers Edition

About Client

HiteJinro is No.1 distiller in South Korea and the world's leading producer of soju. The main products are Chamisul Soju—the world’s best-selling spirit & Hite Beer — the most popular beer in South Korea.

Project Description

Hite Beer holds a partnership with the Los Angeles Dodgers and launches a special edition every year for the LA Dodgers fans. This special edition beer is sold only in the Los Angeles area. Therefore, the promotion was mainly aimed at local residents.

Hite Dodgers Edition package design shows the features of the LA Dodgers. Not only baseball fans but also the general public loved this special package.

The promotion information and game information were provided via social media. By hosting the sample events at the Dodgers Stadium, brand loyalty and familiarity were increased dramatically. Hite Beer successfully supported the heat of the stadium and have spread its reputation as an easy to drink foreign beer.

I worked closely with the marketing department to find the best way to promote the campaign. I have produced the ads in various formats such as newspaper ad, poster, coupon, standing banner, POP display, LED signage, elevator wraps, SNS, app ad, t-shirt, and more. Because of the tight schedule and match results are unpredictable, time management was another key point to ensure this project success.
