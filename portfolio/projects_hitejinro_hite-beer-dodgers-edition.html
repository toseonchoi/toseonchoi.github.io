Go Blue! Go Hite!

Hite Beer Dodgers Edition

About Client

HiteJinro is the leading distiller in South Korea and the world's top producer of soju. Known for its flagship products, Chamisul Soju, the world’s best-selling spirit, and Hite Beer, South Korea’s most popular beer, HiteJinro has solidified its reputation in the global spirits industry.

Project Description

Hite Beer partners with the Los Angeles Dodgers to launch a special edition beer each year for Dodgers fans in the LA area. The promotion is aimed primarily at local residents.

I designed the Hite Dodgers Edition package to reflect the team’s features, appealing not only to baseball fans but also the general public. Promotion information and game updates were shared via social media, and sample events at Dodgers Stadium helped boost brand loyalty.

I collaborated closely with the marketing department to determine the best promotional strategies. I produced a variety of materials, including social media, newspaper ads, posters, coupons, banners, LED signage, and more. Time management was crucial due to tight schedules and unpredictable game outcomes.
