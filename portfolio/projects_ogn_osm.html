OGN — OGN Supermatch: Alpha (OSM)

About Client

OGN is the world’s first professional eSports TV channel, a division of CJ ENM, and has been a leader in the eSports industry for over 19 years. Pioneering the world’s first eSports league, OGN continues to set the standard with top-tier competitions for games like StarCraft, League of Legends, Overwatch, and PUBG. With its programming reaching over 150 countries, OGN has played a major role in bringing eSports to a global audience.

Project Description

OGN opened the first dedicated battle royale studio in the U.S., equipped with state-of-the-art technology. To celebrate, they hosted the OGN Supermatch, an exciting eSports tournament with top players and streamers worldwide.

As a graphic designer, I created promotional materials for social media and YouTube, including event branding, player profiles, and tournament schedules. Working in real-time broadcasting added excitement to the role, allowing me to contribute my skills and help drive the project's success.
