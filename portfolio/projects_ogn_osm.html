OGN — OGN Supermatch: Alpha (OSM)

About Client

OGN is the world's first professional eSport TV channel, a division of CJ ENM. OGN has been making history in the eSports industry for over 19 years. They created the world's first Esports league and continue to lead the way with the world's best eSports Leagues featuring popular games such as StarCraft, Starcraft 2, League of Legends, Overwatch, PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds, and more. Through their programming, OGN has brought the love of video games and eSports to households across more than 150 countries.

Project Description

OGN's opened the first dedicated battle royale studio in the U.S., equipped with cutting-edge technology and production capabilities. To celebrate the grand opening of the new studio, OGN is organizing the OGN Supermatch, an epic esports tournament featuring top professional players and popular streamers from around the world with a diverse range of games and unique rules.

As a graphic designer on the project, I created various promotional materials to bring the brand to life on social media and YouTube. From designing the event's DNA to creating graphics for player profiles and tournament schedules, I crafted each element with precision and care to capture the essence of this electrifying gaming experience. Working in real-time broadcasting added an extra layer of excitement to the job, allowing me to showcase my skills and contribute to the project's success.
