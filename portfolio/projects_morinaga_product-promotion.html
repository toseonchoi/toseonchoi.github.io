Morinaga Product Promotion

About Client

Morinaga is a renowned manufacturer of soy-based products, including the popular Mori-Nu Silken Tofu, sold worldwide. The company also produces Silken Soy Puree, a key ingredient for industrial and food service use. With a commitment to quality, Morinaga brings nutritious and versatile soy products to consumers around the globe.

Project Description

Morinaga produces healthy, organic, non-GMO products, including Mori-Nu Silken Tofu. The advertisements highlighted key nutritional benefits and provided recipes that showcased the versatility of the products, helping consumers create delicious dishes.

Collaborating with the creative director and art director, I designed and formatted the graphic content, ensuring the nutritional details and recipe information were effectively conveyed to the audience.
