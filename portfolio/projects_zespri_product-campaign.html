Zespri Product Campaign

About Client

Zespri is the world's largest kiwifruit marketer, owned by 2,500 New Zealand kiwifruit growers. The company sells to over 54 countries, handling about 30% of global kiwifruit trade. Zespri’s mission is to provide fresh, nutritious, and delicious kiwifruit year-round, ensuring consumers have access to high-quality produce at any time.

Project Description

Zespri aims to provide high-quality, nutritious kiwifruit packed with vitamin C, potassium, and folate. Advertisements highlighted its delicious, juicy taste and the easy health benefits of consuming kiwifruit.

Targeting primarily Asian markets and secondarily general audiences, ads were created in various languages and formats, including newspapers, magazines, standing banners, and web banners.

I executed various advertising materials, ensuring the main visuals were mouth-watering and conveyed the fresh, juicy appeal of the product through detailed, time-intensive design work.
