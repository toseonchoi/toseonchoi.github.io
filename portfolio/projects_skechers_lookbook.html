Skechers Lookbook

About Client

Skechers USA is a global footwear brand offering lifestyle and high-performance shoes for men, women, and children. The company is dedicated to developing and marketing top-quality footwear that caters to a wide range of preferences and needs. As one of the largest footwear brands in the world, Skechers USA continues to push the boundaries of innovation and style, while maintaining a commitment to exceptional quality and customer satisfaction.

Project Description

As part of Skechers' seasonal marketing efforts for wholesalers, retailers, and general customers, I was responsible for creating lookbooks that introduced new styles for the season and focused on specific divisions and products.

In this role, I served as both a creative designer and project manager, overseeing the entire process from start to finish. This included developing the concept, ensuring the accuracy of all product information, and collaborating with photographers, retouchers, copywriters, and a production manager. Through my efforts, Skechers was able to produce engaging and visually appealing lookbooks that effectively showcased their latest products and helped drive sales.
