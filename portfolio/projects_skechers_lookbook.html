Skechers Lookbook

About Client

Skechers USA is a global leader in lifestyle and athletic footwear and apparel for men, women, and kids, featuring innovative comfort technologies. Committed to delivering high-quality products, Skechers caters to diverse tastes and needs with exceptional style and comfort.

As one of the world’s largest footwear brands, Skechers continues to push the boundaries of style and functionality while maintaining its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Project Description

As part of Skechers' seasonal marketing strategy for wholesalers and retailers, I was responsible for creating lookbooks that showcased specific divisions, introduced new styles, and highlighted products and technologies.

In this role, I served as both a creative designer and project manager, overseeing the entire process from concept development to final production. This involved ensuring product accuracy and collaborating closely with the product team, photographers, retouchers, copywriters, and production managers. My efforts resulted in engaging and visually appealing lookbooks that effectively showcased Skechers' upcoming products and supported sales initiatives.
