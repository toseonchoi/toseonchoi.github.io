Skechers Lookbook

About Client

Skechers is one of the biggest footwear brands worldwide — lifestyle and high-performance footwear company, develops and markets to more than 3,000 styles for men, women and children.

Project Description

Every season, Skechers produces lookbooks to introduce new styles for the season. This focuses on specific division, provides detailed information about shoes, and guides it's styling.

Not only as a creative designer, but also as a person in charge, I was responsible for manages the entire process — developing concept, correcting proper information, worked with photographers, retouchers, copywriters, and a production manager.
