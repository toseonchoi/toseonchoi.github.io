Most Accurate Golf Rangefinders!

GolfBuddy Promotion / Trade Show

About Client

GolfBuddy is a leading innovator in the golf rangefinder industry with a primary goal to provide the most accurate and innovative golf rangefinders in the market. GolfBuddy has the most accurate course database with physically verified courses across the world.

Project Description

GolfBuddy offers the special promotion for the limited time throughout all seasons and occasions. GolfBuddy also participates in PGA Merchandise Show with opportunities for business-to-business every year.

The beginning of the year advertising, spring promotion marked the beginning of the golf season. The ads promoted the special price and gift event for the person worthy of it such as the mothers, fathers, and labors. Moreover, advertisement executed for the holiday season and headlined whenever a new product line was released.

PGA Merchandise Show booth graphics theme was the futuristic and high tech. Robots and surrealistic colors maximized the concept.

I worked as an exclusive designer of GolfBuddy client. I created, executed, and delivered all creative materials to the client and the media.
