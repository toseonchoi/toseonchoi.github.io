Most Accurate Golf Rangefinders!

GolfBuddy Promotion / Trade Show

About Client

GolfBuddy is a leader in the golf rangefinder industry, known for offering highly accurate and innovative devices. The company boasts the most precise course database, featuring physically verified courses worldwide, to ensure golfers have the best possible information for their game.

Project Description

GolfBuddy offers special promotions throughout the year, coinciding with key seasons and occasions. The company also participates in the PGA Merchandise Show, showcasing B2B opportunities.

For spring promotions, I designed ads highlighting special offers for holidays such as Mother's Day, Father's Day and Labor Day, along with new product line releases. At the PGA Merchandise Show, I created futuristic, high-tech booth graphics using robots and surreal colors to enhance the theme.

As the lead designer, I created, executed, and delivered all creative materials to the client and media.
