HiteJinro Social Media

About Client

HiteJinro is No.1 distiller in South Korea and the world's leading producer of soju. The main products are Chamisul Soju—the world’s best-selling spirit & Hite Beer — the most popular beer in South Korea.

Project Description

Nowadays, social media became a most powerful marketing tool. Our team and client realized that the existing social media pages need to improve in a more unique way over traditional methods, and turned the marketing strategy into the interactive marketing.

I worked as a responsible producer of SNS graphics and collaborated with the marketing department. We chose the most efficient social media format for each project, including image, animated image, video, or live.

In order to increase the local audience's response, we promoted HiteJinro's products and brand by celebrating the local anniversaries. I created the special package designs for SNS, and it successfully attracted viewers and led to many likes and comments.

We also provided interesting games and recipes, introduced unique Korean culture and stories related to products, offered giveaways and contests, and announced events that people would experience the products in person.
