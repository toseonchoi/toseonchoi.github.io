Cedars-Sinai Outdoor Promotion

About Client

Cedars-Sinai is a nonprofit academic healthcare organization serving the diverse Los Angeles community. Cedars-Sinai serves more than 1 million people every year in over 40 locations, with more than 4,500 physicians and nurses and 1,500 research projects in motion. Cedars-Sinai is dedicated to providing care for everyone who needs it.

Project Description

Cedars-Sinai is located in the Beverly Grove neighborhood of Koreatown LA, is very accessible from the Koreatown. Cedars-Sinai started Korean language services and provides the detailed consultation on insurance benefit.

The purpose of the project was encouraging Koreans to visit the Cedars-Sinai Medical Center and to get quality care without feeling uncomfortable or language barrier.

To target the progressive Koreans, our team deliberately selected the shopping mall that Koreans visit the most. As creative, I had produced and managed outdoor advertisements such as light boxes and elevator wraps.

The dvertisements emphasized the fact that Cedars-Sinai is closer than expected and that Korean language services are being provided. It also encouraged telephone inquiries about health insurance coverage and other support.
