Cedars-Sinai Outdoor Promotion

About Client

Cedars-Sinai is a nonprofit healthcare organization in Los Angeles, providing care to over 1 million people annually. With over 4,500 physicians and nurses, and 1,500 active research projects, it focuses on delivering high-quality, accessible healthcare. It serves a diverse community, with a commitment to ensuring healthcare is available to everyone who needs it.

Project Description

Cedars-Sinai, located in Koreatown, LA, offers Korean language services and detailed insurance consultations to make healthcare more accessible. The goal was to encourage Korean residents to visit the medical center and receive quality care without language barriers. I produced and managed outdoor ads like light boxes and elevator wraps in popular shopping malls. Additionally, I worked on creating a TV commercial emphasizing Cedars-Sinai’s proximity, Korean language services, and encouraging inquiries about health insurance coverage and support.
