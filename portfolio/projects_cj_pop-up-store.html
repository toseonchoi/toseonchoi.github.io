The Live New Experience

CJ Pop-up Store

About Client

CJ is a leading South Korean conglomerate that began as a food manufacturing company under Samsung. It has since grown into a global powerhouse across four key sectors: Food & Food Service, Biotechnology, Entertainment & Media, and Retail & Logistics.

To expand its international reach, CJ established its U.S. headquarters, enhancing its influence in North America. With iconic brands like CJ Entertainment, CGV, 4DX, KCON, Mnet, tvN, TVING, Studio Dragon, Bibigo, Tous Les Jours, and Olive Young, CJ continues to innovate and share Korean culture worldwide.

Project Description

I had the privilege of working on The Live New Experience, a pop-up store event by CJ showcasing Korean lifestyle brands. The event featured the latest K-beauty products curated by Olive Young, K-style food art by Bibigo, and exclusive beauty collaborations with SEP Beauty, along with K-entertainment merchandise in partnership with K-Pop group WannaOne.

As the design lead, I created captivating exterior and interior design that enhanced the immersive experience. I collaborated with marketing teams, event planners, and vendors to ensure cohesive visuals. Additionally, I designed promotional materials and coordinated influencer meet-and-greet sessions to amplify brand engagement and promote Korean culture.
