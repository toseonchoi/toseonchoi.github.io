The Live New Experience

CJ Pop-up Store

About Client

CJ is a dynamic and innovative conglomerate holding company headquartered in South Korea. Originally a branch of Samsung, CJ has since evolved into a separate entity with four core business portfolios: Food & Food Service, Biotechnology, Entertainment & Media, and Retail & Logistics.

As a leading player in the Korean market, CJ is renowned for its impressive portfolio of brands and services, including CJ Entertainment, CGV, 4DX, KCON, OGN, Bibigo, Tous Les Jours, Olive Young, and more. With a strong focus on innovation, CJ is committed to bringing Korean cuisine and culture to the global stage and currently operates in over 40 countries.

Project Description

I had the privilege of working on The Live New Experience, a special pop-up store event created by CJ. The event was designed to provide a unique and immersive experience for customers through Korean lifestyle brands, featuring the latest K-beauty products curated by Olive Young, exclusive K-style food art installations by Bibigo, and beauty collaborations with SEP Beauty. This event also showcased K-entertainment merchandise created in collaboration with the popular K-Pop boy band WannaOne.

As the design lead, I collaborated closely with the marketing team, event planners, and vendors to create visually stunning exterior and interior displays that effectively showcased the products and created an unforgettable experience for visitors.

In addition to designing the exhibition, I produced various marketing materials and prepared meet-and-greet sessions with influencers to promote the Korean Wave and immerse customers further in Korean culture.
