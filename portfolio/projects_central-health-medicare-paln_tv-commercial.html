Aging Is Beautiful!

Central Health Medicare Plan TV Commercial

About Client

Central Health Medicare Plan is a health insurance provider serving approximately 13,000 members across L.A. County, Orange County, and San Bernardino County. The agency has been recognized with the Senior Gold Choice Award from 2011 to 2013.

Project Description

Central Health Medicare Plan offers insurance plans tailored to seniors and runs TV commercials for branding and enrollment campaigns each year. Ads were tailored in Chinese, Korean, and Hispanic languages to target diverse consumer groups.

For branding, the commercials emphasized themes like happy retirement and family love, while the enrollment campaign focused on coverage details and language-specific services.

As an art director, I assisted the director by setting up custom mise-en-scène, managing props and wardrobe, and working on post-production motion graphics. It was a fantastic opportunity to collaborate with the cast and crew.
