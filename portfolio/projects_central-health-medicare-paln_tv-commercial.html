Aging Is Beautiful!

Central Health Medicare Plan TV Commercial

About Client

Central Health Medicare Plan is a health insurance agency. The Central Health Care Plan holds about 13,000 members in L.A. County, Orange County, and San Bernardino County. Central Health Medicare Plan won the Senior Gold Choice Award from 2011 to 2013.

Project Description

Central Health Medicare Plan provides valuable insurance plan especially for the elderly and conducts TV commercials for their branding and enrollment every year.

In order to secure Chinese, Korean and Hispanic consumer groups, the advertisements released three language versions of TV commercials.

For branding, TV commercials broadcasted full of positive meanings such as pleasant elderly life, carefree retirement, and happy family love.

For enrollment campaign, TV commercials dealt with insurance coverages, Korean language services, professional staffs, and quality services to encourage the registrations.

I assisted TV commercial as an art director and as a prop manager from the pre to post production. I also participated in the post-processing of motion graphics for the enrollment campaign. This project was great opportunity to work with the cast and the crew.
