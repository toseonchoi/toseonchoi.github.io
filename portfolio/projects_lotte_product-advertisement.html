Fresh Trevi & Sweet Pepero!

Lotte Product Advertisement

About Client

Lotte is one of largest multinational conglomerates in South Korea and Japan. Lotte has become one of food and lifestyle business which provides the wide range of consumer-oriented products and services.

Project Description

Lotte sponsored South Korean travel-reality show, Youth Over Flowers, and placed Trevi product. They pushed forward the global marketing while TV show became popular. Trevi marketing was extended to the print advertisement, and standing banner displayed on the market. The cool, thrilling, product image making maximized the advertising effect in the hot summer season.

Pepero, Lotte’s another well-known product, hits highest sales rate during Pepero Day season. Pepero Day is a special day held on November 11 in South Korea. Friends, lovers, and other acquaintances exchange Pepero snack to express their friendship, love, and appreciation for each another. To spread this unique K-culture to the United States, Lotte prepared a free gift event of Pepero.

I produced various kinds of advertising material and delivered it to the clients and the media.
